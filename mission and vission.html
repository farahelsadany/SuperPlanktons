Mission and Vission

Our team, the Super Planktons team, believes in the mind-blowing superpowers of our magnificent planet. Thus, for the sake of saving Earth and its superpowers, our challenge mainly focuses on increasing the level of people's awareness and knowledge about the Earth to encourage them to be more concerned about protecting the Earth's superpowers. That's why we designed this website, containing all the targeted contents "like; images, videos, picture libraries, document libraries… etc.", to give our users the opportunity to know everything about phytoplanktons which have a vital role and impact on our planet.
